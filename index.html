Thank you to all our conference participants for a successful ICST 2015

Welcome to the official website of the International Conference on Science and Technology 2015

On behalf of the organizing committee, we are pleased to invite you to submit your original research article in the 1st International Conference on Science and Technology (ICST 2015). This conference is the 1st Universitas Gadjah Mada annual scientific meeting on science and technology. The topics covered in the conference include, but not limited to, mathematics and statistics, natural sciences, computer science, applied science, and engineering.

The ICST 2015 is organized by Universitas Gadjah Mada and will be held in Yogyakarta, the home to the Javanese culture. The aim of the conference is to provide a platform to the researchers and practitioners from both academia as well as industry to meet and share cutting-edge progress in the fields of science and engineering, or technology in general. Graduate students, especially Ph.D. students, are welcomed to submit their thesis works.

All submitted papers will be reviewed by at least two reviewers to ensure the scientific quality. All the accepted papers will be published in the AIP Conference Proceeding. Moreover, the selected papers will be published in a regular journal “Indonesian Journal of Chemistry”. These publishing companies are now indexed by Scopus.

In addition, we plan to organize post-conference tour for foreign participants who are interested to join.
We look forward to meeting you in Yogyakarta, Indonesia.

Sincerely,
Kuwat Triyana
Chairman of ICST 2015
